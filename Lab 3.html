Lab 3

Task

Write a complete program that asks the user for a "formula." A formula is of the form "number operation number" where "number" is some decimal number, and "operation" is one of +, -, *, and / (you can use a char variable to get the operation, since they are always just one symbol). An example is the formula 4.3 + 3.2. Your program should compute the answer to the formula and print it (with fixed precision 3). Be sure to use a switch statement to determine which operation was entered.

Examples

Enter a simple mathematical formula: 0.0 + 0.0
Answer: 0.000

Enter a simple mathematical formula: 123.45 - 123.45
Answer: 0.000

Enter a simple mathematical formula: 5.67 * -84.27
Answer: -477.811

Enter a simple mathematical formula: -3497.45 / 354.3385045
Answer: -9.870

For the following example, "inf" is automatically printed whenever you divided a double by 0.0. You do not need a special check for 0.0.

Enter a simple mathematical formula: 35 / 0
Answer: inf

Same with "nan" ("not a number"); it is automatically printed when 0.0 is divided by 0.0.

Enter a simple mathematical formula: 0.0 / 0.0
Answer: nan
